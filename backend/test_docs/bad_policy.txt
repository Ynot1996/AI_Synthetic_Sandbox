QUICKSURE PERSONAL ACCIDENT INSURANCE — POLICY SUMMARY

Thank you for choosing QuickSure. This document summarises your cover.

WHAT IS COVERED
You are covered for accidental death up to £5,000 and hospitalisation up to £500 per night
(maximum 3 nights). Certain events may be excluded — see the full policy booklet (Appendix F).

MONTHLY PREMIUM
£24.99 per month. Payments will be taken automatically each month.

INTEREST AND CREDIT
Where you elect to pay annually by credit, a small finance charge applies. Details available
on request. Representative APR varies.

AUTO-RENEWAL
Your policy renews automatically each year. If you do not wish to renew please notify us.

EXCLUSIONS
Pre-existing conditions are excluded. Adventure sports are excluded. Acts of negligence
may be excluded. Full list of exclusions is in Appendix F of the policy document which can
be requested by post.

CANCELLATION
To cancel your policy please call our customer service team. Lines are open Monday to Friday,
9am–5pm. A cancellation fee of £35 applies in the first 12 months.

COMPLAINTS
We take complaints seriously. Please write to our Compliance Manager at our registered address.

TARGET MARKET
This product is designed for adults aged 18 to 35. It is particularly popular with people
who are new to financial products and those with irregular employment patterns.

*APR and full terms available on request. Finance charges, late payment fees and early
termination conditions are described in the policy booklet.
